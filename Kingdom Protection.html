Kingdom Protection

tags: Apps

You are the best strategist and your mission is to protect your kingdom. Put the right launchers at the right place, right time, to stop your enemies from destroy your home. ## Features - 4 types of missile launchers: Golden one, Silver one, Copper one and Iron one. Each has different cost and power of damage; - Different types of enemies; - Game center integrated. You can challenge top strategists from all over the world;
