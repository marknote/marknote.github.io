MarkNote

tags: Apps


MarkNote is an iOS app to help you to take notes with Markdown. It can help to manage your notes. You can even use it to maintain a blog site.

FEATURES

  • Embed photos and images into note

  • Live preview while writing
  • Auto save
  • Send mail with rich format
  • Swipe to delete a note
  • Syntax highlighting
  • Review and edit your notes from any web browsers or smart devices
  • Sync your notes into iCloud drive, and share with all your devices. You can read and edit your notes with marknote app on iPhone/iPad or marknotes on mac

You have complete control to your data.
