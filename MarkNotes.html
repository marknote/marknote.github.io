Why MarkNote?

Sometimes you can do more with less

 Modern documentation apps are powerful, but they can overload you with choices. Hundreds of settings, millions of styles, and infinite layers all lead to complex interfaces that make simple documentation time consuming and frustrating.

Marknote takes a different approach

 It gives you the right balance of power and control to documentation effortlessly. The focus stays where it belongs: on your creations, not the tools. Controls stay "at the fringes" until needed, but still provide the flexibility to change as needed.

Features

Clean and intuitive writing

  • Powerful, yet simple text editor
  • Comprehensive and truly flexible multi-format export, you can export your notes into HTML or PDF format, you can even use it to build static web sites
  • Live preview while writing
  • Syntax highlighting for Markdown and code

Safe and convenient document management
  • Your notes synced into iCloud drive, and share among all your devices.
  • You can read and edit your notes with marknote app on iPhone/iPad or marknotes on mac
  • Organize your documents with tags
  • Instant search on your notes
