ZhugeNu

tags: Apps


Play the medieval missile launcher invented by Zhuge Liang, the greatest militarist during the Three Kingdoms period. You have 4 types of missile launchers. Each has different cost and power of damage. Put the right launchers at the right place, right time, to stop your enemies from occupying your basecamp. ## Features - 4 types of missile launchers: Golden one, Silver one, Copper one and Iron one.
