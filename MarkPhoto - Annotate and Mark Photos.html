MarkPhoto - Annotate and Mark Photos

tags: Apps


Sometimes you want to freely annotate on your photos to express your ideas;
Sometimes you have concerns before sharing your photos just because they carry too much information;

MarkPhoto is the only image marking and annotation tool you need on iPhone. It helps to easily mark any sensitive parts of your images before sharing. You can annotate on your photos as well. It helps to make interesting images, at the same time, to protect you privacy.

Features:

  • Change color and stroke thickness;
  • Unlimited undo;
  • One click share annotated photo to twitter, mail, notes
